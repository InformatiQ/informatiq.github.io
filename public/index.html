Coaching

What is it?

Coaching is a form of development in which an experienced person, called a coach, supports a learner, called coachee, in achieving a specific personal or professional goal by providing training and guidance.
In simple terms, coaching is an opportunity for the coachee to take the time to work on themselves with the help of a coach.
The coach helps the coachee in gaining clarity on their goals. Be mindful of their current ways. Paint a picture of the destination and support them in getting there.

How does it go?

A coaching program has a start and an end.

  • It starts with the coachee having a topic to work on that is important to them and their development.
  • A discovery session, in which the coach and the coachee learn a little about each other, break the ice. They will also start to discover the topic. The purpose is for both to get clarity on the topic.
  • In the next session, the coach will propose a coaching program that is customized to the coachee that runs over several sessions (4-6 months). They will discuss and refine it together. The program will have clear goals and exercises to help the coachee achieve them.
  • Finally, a closing session to review and celebrate the change.

Yet the journey doesn’t stop there, the coach merely helps the coachee get on the path to achieving their goals.
After the program is over, the work continues and the coachee continues on their journey of growth without the coach.
